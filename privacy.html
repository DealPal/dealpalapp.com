1. INTRODUCTION
1.1 Deal Pal Inc. ("Deal Pal," "we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Deal Pal™ mobile application and related services ("App").
1.2 By downloading or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy and consent to the practices described herein.
1.3 This Policy complies with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and other applicable Canadian privacy laws.
1.4 If you do not agree with this Policy, please discontinue use of the App immediately.
⸻
2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We collect the following categories of information to operate and improve the App:
(a) Account Information
• Name (optional)
• Username
• Email address
• Password (hashed/encrypted)
• Optional profile photo
• Account creation date
(b) Location Data
• Precise GPS location (with explicit permission only)
• Approximate location
• Location accuracy details
Location data is used exclusively to show nearby deals. The App does not access precise location without your explicit opt-in via device permissions.
(c) User-Generated Content
• Deal posts (title, price, store, quantity, location info)
• Photos uploaded to the App
• Comments, replies, likes, and interaction metrics
(d) Device Information
• Device model
• OS version
• IP address
• Device identifier
• Mobile network info
• App version
(e) Usage Data and Analytics
• Pages/features used
• Session duration
• Timestamps of activity
• Crash logs
• Performance data
• Search queries
(f) Cookies & Local Storage
• Session cookies
• Analytics cookies
• Local storage for app settings
You may disable cookies in device settings, but some features may not function properly.
